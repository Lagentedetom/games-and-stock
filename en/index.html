📜 Trading Rules
01
Entry Rule
If Trends has been above 30/100 for 4+ weeks and still growing, entry signal 60-90 days before launch.
02
Exit Signal
If hype explodes in last 2 weeks (10 to 100), too late for PRE-launch entry.
03
Optimal Company
Mid-cap, where the game represents >30% of future revenue. Avoid giants where impact is diluted.
04
Cyberpunk Factor
Extreme hype + expectations/reality gap = crash. Don't blindly trust Trends acceleration.
